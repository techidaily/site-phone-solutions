Not! This message does not necessarily mean that you have blocked yourself or there is something wrong with your device. [**_How to distinguish "No Location Found" and "Location Not Available"?_**](https://drfone.wondershare.com/virtual-location/no-location-found-vs-location-not-available.html)

"No Location Found" means that the Find My app is unable to locate the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device being tracked. This can happen for a variety of reasons, such as if the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device is turned off, not connected to the internet, or if location services are disabled.

"Location Not Available" means that the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device is not currently sharing its location with the Find My app. This can happen if the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device is in Airplane mode or if the user has turned off location sharing for the Find My app. It can also happen if there is a temporary issue with the iCloud servers.

In summary, "No Location Found" means the app could not locate the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device, while "Location Not Available" means that the Samsung Galaxy F15 5G device is not currently sharing its location.
