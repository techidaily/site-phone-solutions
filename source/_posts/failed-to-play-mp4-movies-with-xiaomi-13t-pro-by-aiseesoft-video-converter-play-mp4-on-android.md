Although Xiaomi 13T Pro is compatible with MP4 video files, it also requires the MP4 file to be encoded in the way that is recognized by your particular device model. Some MP4 videos will not play on Xiaomi 13T Pro due to the failure of codec recognition. In that case, you will need to convert the files to Xiaomi 13T Pro supported H.264 MP4 format.
