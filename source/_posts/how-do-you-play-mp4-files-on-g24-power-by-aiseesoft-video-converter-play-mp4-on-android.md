The reason is that MP4 is a container format which may contain multiple video and audio streams encoded with various codecs. Although Motorola G24 Power is compatible with MP4 video files, it also requires the MP4 file to be encoded in the way that is recognized by your particular device model. Some MP4 videos will not play on Motorola G24 Power due to the failure of codec recognition. In that case, you will need to convert the files to Motorola G24 Power supported H.264 MP4 format.
